Who am I? I'm an engineer who does his best to make sure things go "kaboom" as little as possible (unless they're intended to). I love seeing systems move for the first time! I graduated with high distinction from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with degrees in Robotics Engineering and Electrical & Computer Engineering, before working towards my Master's degree in Robotics Engineering. I was the recruitment officer for WPI's robotics engineering honors society, a member of WPI's orchestra, and vice-president of the WPI tennis team. I've held internships at MIT, MITRE, Sensata Technologies, and most recently Vecna Robotics, and can't wait to get out into the industry full-time in December 2020. I also speak Russian and am working am (slowly) working on my Spanish.
